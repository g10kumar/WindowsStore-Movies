About Us

The Newspapers app for Windows 8 offers the top newspapers in the world at your fingertips. You can view the top newspapers in any of the more than 150 countries and also the 50 states of the United States. The countries are organized by regions. Select the country and then select the newspaper. This will open the Newspapers website in a browser.


The best way to view the newspapers is to put the app in Snapped view and then browse the different newspapers. Its like your virtual shelf.
