About Us

The Top American Speeches app for Windows 8 offers the top speeches made in American history by American speakers. The speeches are ordered by the speech title. To use the app, browse through the speeches and speakers and select a speaker to display the speech text. We are planning on bringing videos of these speeches also in a future version.


If you have any issues or have a suggestion for the app, please email us at support@daksatech.com.


The app was developed by DaksaTech. We have many more apps for Windows 8 as well as Windows Phone.
